Validation Rules

Required fields: question_type, question_text, correct_answer
MCQ: options required (semi-colon separated)
Match: pairs required (pipe and colon separated)
Difficulty: easy, medium, hard

Quick Format Guide

MCQ Format:

options: "Option A;Option B;Option C;Option D"
correct_answer: "Option B"

Match Format:

pairs: "Left 1:Right 1|Left 2:Right 2|Left 3:Right 3"

True/False Format:

correct_answer: "TRUE" or "FALSE"

Common Issues to Avoid

  • Typos in options and answers
  • Missing required fields for specific question types
  • Incorrect separator usage (; for options, | for pairs)
  • Case sensitivity in True/False answers
