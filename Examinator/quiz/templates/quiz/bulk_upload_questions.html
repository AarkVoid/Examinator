CSV Template and Guidelines

Use this template to correctly structure your questions. Download Sample CSV

Required Headers:


question_type,question_text,options,correct_answer,pairs,board,class_name,subject,chapter,section,difficulty,marks,explanation,max_words,is_case_sensitive

Supported **question_type** values: `MCQ`, `Match`, `FillBlank`, `TrueFalse`, `ShortAnswer`.

  • For `MCQ`: Use the **options** column (separated by semi-colons: `Choice A;Choice B;...`) and the **correct_answer** column.
  • For `Match`: Use the **pairs** column (separated by pipe and colon: `Left A:Right 1|Left B:Right 2|...`).
  • For `FillBlank` / `TrueFalse` / `ShortAnswer`: Use the **correct_answer** column.
